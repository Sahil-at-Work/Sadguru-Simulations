CONCEPT CHECK

Electromagnetic Induction: Mechanical energy rotates the coil $ABCD$ in a magnetic field, changing the magnetic flux passing through it.
Fleming's Right-Hand Rule: Align your thumb (Motion), index finger (Field N➔S), and middle finger (Induced Current) to map induction vectors.
AC vs. DC: AC uses isolated **Slip Rings** changing output directions every half-rotation. DC uses a **Split Ring / Commutator** keeping the output current constant in one direction.
